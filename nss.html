NATIONAL SERVICE SCHEME

Dr. Dharmendra Kumar (Conveyner NSS)

The National Service Scheme (NSS) at TNB Law College is a vibrant and integral part of the institution, dedicated to fostering a sense of social responsibility and community engagement among students. The NSS unit aims to develop the personality of students through community service and instill the values of empathy, leadership, and social justice.

Objectives of NSS:

1. Community Engagement: Promote active participation in community service activities to address societal issues.
2. Holistic Development: Facilitate the overall development of students by providing opportunities for personal growth and skill enhancement.
3. Leadership and Teamwork: Encourage students to develop leadership qualities and work effectively in teams.
4. Awareness and Advocacy: Raise awareness about social, cultural, and environmental issues and advocate for positive change.

NSS Initiatives at TNB Law College

Activities and Initiatives The NSS Department of TNB Law College organizes a variety of activities and initiatives throughout the academic year:
1. Community Service Camps: Conduct regular camps in rural and urban areas to address community needs such as health, education, and sanitation.
2. Health and Hygiene Campaigns: Organize health camps, blood donation drives, and awareness campaigns on hygiene and sanitation.
3. Environmental Conservation: Initiate tree plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, and workshops on environmental sustainability.
4. Legal Awareness Programs: Conduct legal literacy camps to educate the community about their rights and responsibilities under the law.
5. Social Welfare Activities: Engage in activities like visiting orphanages, old age homes, and supporting marginalized sections of society.
6. Skill Development Workshops: Offer workshops and training sessions on life skills, leadership, and entrepreneurship.
