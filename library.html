TNB Law College Library

Treasure Trove

TNB Law College Library


The Law Library at TNB Law College, Bhagalpur is a treasure trove of legal knowledge, offering a vast collection of physical books dedicated to various law courses.
Spanning across different legal disciplines, the library houses an extensive range of textbooks, reference materials, case studies, and legal journals, meticulously curated to support the academic needs of our students and faculty. With comfortable reading areas and state-of-the-art facilities, the Law Library provides an ideal environment for in-depth study and research.
Whether you're exploring foundational legal concepts or delving into specialized areas of law, the Law Library is your gateway to a wealth of legal resources, enriching your academic journey at TNB Law College, Bhagalpur.

A Few Prominent Highlights of our Library :

"Introduction to the Constitution of India" by Durga Das Basu
"Indian Constitutional Law" by M.P. Jain
"Principles of Statutory Interpretation" by Justice G.P. Singh
"Constitutional Law of India" by Dr. J.N. Pandey
"Law of Contract and Specific Relief" by Avtar Singh
"Indian Penal Code" by K.D. Gaur
"Environmental Law in India" by P. Leelakrishnan
"Intellectual Property Law in India" by V.K. Ahuja
"Labour and Industrial Law" by P.L. Malik
"Law of Torts" by R.K. Bangia
