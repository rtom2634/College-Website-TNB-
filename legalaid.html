Legal Aid Clinic

Dr. Prashant Kumar(Conveyner Legal Aid Clinic)

The Legal Aid Clinic at TNB Law College is a dedicated initiative aimed at providing free legal assistance to those in need while offering practical legal education to students. The Clinic serves as a bridge between the legal knowledge acquired in classrooms and its application in real-world scenarios. By involving students in the process of offering legal aid, the Clinic aims to foster a sense of social responsibility and enhance practical legal skills.


Objectives of Legal Aid Clinic:


1. Provide Free Legal Assistance: Offer pro bono legal services to underprivileged and marginalized sections of society.
2. Practical Legal Education: Enhance the practical legal skills of students through direct involvement in legal aid cases.
3. Raise Legal Awareness: Educate the community about their legal rights and obligations through various outreach programs.
4. Promote Social Justice: Work towards ensuring access to justice for all, particularly the underrepresented and vulnerable groups.
5. Encourage Pro Bono Work: Instill the values of public service and pro bono work among future legal professionals.

Initiatives at TNB Law College:

To align with the standards of the Indian Education System, TNB Law College has implemented several key initiatives through Legal Aid Clinic:
1. Legal Literacy Camps: Organize camps in rural and urban areas to educate people about basic legal rights and procedures.
2. Workshops and Seminars: Conduct educational workshops and seminars on pertinent legal issues and developments.
3. Pro Bono Week: A dedicated week where students and faculty provide intensive legal aid services to the community.
4. Collaborations: Partner with local NGOs, bar associations, and other organizations to enhance the reach and effectiveness of legal aid services.
5. Client Counseling Competitions: Host competitions to improve the counseling skills of law students and prepare them for real-world legal practice.

Services Offered at Tnb Law College:

The Legal Aid Clinic provides a wide range of legal services, including but not limited to:
1. Legal Consultation: Offer free legal advice and consultation on various legal issues, including family law, property disputes, consumer rights, and more.
2. Legal Representation: Represent clients in court who are unable to afford legal representation.
3. Documentation Assistance: Assist with the preparation and filing of legal documents such as affidavits, petitions, and applications.
4. Public Legal Education: Conduct workshops, seminars, and awareness campaigns to educate the public about their legal rights and responsibilities.
5. Advocacy and Outreach: Engage in community outreach programs to identify legal needs and provide necessary support.
