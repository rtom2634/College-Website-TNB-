About Us

Innovative Way To Learn

T.N.B. Law College is a premier institute located in Bhagalpur, Bihar. The institute offers Degree courses including UG programs. These programs are offered in Full-Time mode. Candidate can pursue multiple ranges of courses like LL.B, B.A. LL.B. in the stream of Law. Over the period, T.N.B. Law College has gained expertise through its trained and experienced faculty. The institute provides good facilities all around the campus, such as Auditorium, Hospital / Medical Facilities, Library, Shuttle Service, Wi-Fi Campus, etc.
